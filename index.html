Working Style

How I Work With Product, Engineering and Operations

  • Start with the workflow. The best requirements come from seeing how work actually happens.
  • Protect the roadmap from panic. A painful incident deserves action, but not every exception deserves permanent software.
  • Make reliability visible. Incidents, support signals, and adoption data should shape product priorities.
  • Ship with rollout in mind. Training, SOPs, release sequencing, and feedback loops are part of the product.
