Career OS - AI-enabled Job Search Operating System

A serious job search can become scattered across job boards, notes, CV versions, recruiter messages, and interview prep. The problem was not lack of effort; it was lack of an operating system.

Context

Eric built Career OS for his own job search, with the same product discipline he uses for operations and platform work.

Problem

A serious job search can become scattered across job boards, notes, CV versions, recruiter messages, and interview prep. The problem was not lack of effort; it was lack of an operating system.

What I did

  • Created structured trackers for applications, recruiter CRM, target companies, job scoring, stories, and daily actions.
  • Built local scripts that score roles, generate daily briefs, produce outreach drafts, and create tailored application packets.
  • Added clear safety rules so external actions remain manual and reputation-safe.
  • Used the system to keep applications, follow-ups, stories, and recruiter conversations moving with less manual drift.

Product decisions

  • Optimise for recruiter conversations and interviews, not mass applications.
  • Make the daily workflow simple enough to use every morning.
  • Keep AI in the preparation layer, with Eric approving all external actions.

Result

Created a repeatable workflow that turns job-search activity into scored decisions, tailored assets, prepared outreach, follow-up discipline, and interview-ready stories.

What this proves

  • Eric can design and operate practical AI-assisted workflows.
  • He understands how to turn a messy personal process into a lightweight product system.
  • He can build enough automation to improve focus without creating unnecessary risk.

Proof points

Job scoring engine; tailored CV/cover letter generation; recruiter messaging approval queue; daily sprint output; Notion-ready operating model; LinkedIn content workflow.
